Mobile Management for Mass Casualties.

M3C allows emergency responders to track casualties more effectively, in an effort to expedite disaster recovery and reunite family members with their loved ones.

Learn more »

A product of PennApss XIII.

This product was developed in 48 hours by three Computer Science Students from the University of South Carolina; Maribeth Bottorff, Blakeley Hoffman, and Omar Ansari.

Note:

This product is not meant to be used for real emergencies at this time. Remember, call 911 if this is a real disaster.
